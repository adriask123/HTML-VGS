On launch the game had a few playable factions including the ogre kingdoms who is technicaly a DLC but was available to those who prepurchased the game or bought the game within first week. The factions are: Kislev led by tzarina Katarin, Kostaltyn and Boris Ursus. Daemons of chaos led by the Daemon Prince. Grand cathay led by Miao Ying the storm dragon and Zhao Ming the iron dragon. Nurgle led by Ku`Gath plaguefather. The ogre kingdoms led by Greasus goldtooth and Skrag the slaughterer. Khorne led by Skarbrand the exiled. Slaanesh led by N`Kari. Tzeentch led by Kairos fateweaver.
